Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

At Mobile Smart Teachers Enterprises, we are committed to protecting the privacy and security of our students, parents, and tutors. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information that is necessary to provide our tuition services, including:

  • Contact Information: Names, phone numbers, and email addresses.
  • Student Details: Grade level, school, subjects of interest, and academic performance history.
  • Location Data: Physical address for home tuition services.
  • Communication: Records of our interactions via phone, WhatsApp, or email.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • To match students with the most suitable tutors.
  • To schedule and coordinate tuition sessions.
  • To track academic progress and provide feedback to parents.
  • To process payments and maintain administrative records.
  • To communicate updates about our services.

4. Data Protection

We implement appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your personal information. Access to student data is restricted to authorized staff and the assigned tutor only.

5. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others. We may share student learning goals and grade levels with assigned tutors to facilitate the learning process.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request the deletion of your personal information held by us. If you wish to exercise these rights, please contact us at mobilesmartteachersltd@gmail.com.
