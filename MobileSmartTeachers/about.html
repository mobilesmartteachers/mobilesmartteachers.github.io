Our Story

Built on a Passion for Teaching

Founded with a simple vision, Mobile Smart Teachers Enterprises was established to be the front runner in the provision of high-quality tutoring services and provide fulfilling part time jobs to graduate teachers and other related professionals.

What started as a small team of passionate educators has grown into a premier tuition centre in Lusaka, serving hundreds of students across various syllabuses including ECZ, Cambridge, and CBSE.

Our Mission

Mobile Smart Teachers Enterprises works to provide high-quality holistic educational tutorial services to all the young people in Zambia. We promote academic excellence in learners in order to prepare them for global tasks by not just taeching the school based curriculum but also offer subjects of global demand. We also wish to be the best online platform for tutorial learning for all learners across the world.
